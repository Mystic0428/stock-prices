Item 2. Management’s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

The following discussion and analysis of our financial condition and results of operations should be read in conjunction with our condensed consolidated financial statements and related notes included elsewhere in this Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q.

We are a technology company focused on the development of end-to-end supply chain technology. Our AI-enabled robotic systems transform the movement of goods through supply chains for some of the world’s largest retailers and wholesalers.

Revenue for the three months ended March 28, 2026 was $543 million, compared to $492 million for the three months ended March 29, 2025, representing an increase of approximately 10%. The increase was primarily driven by increased system deployments and software maintenance revenues.

Gross profit for the three months ended March 28, 2026 was $78 million, compared to $65 million for the three months ended March 29, 2025. Gross margin was 14.4% for the three months ended March 28, 2026.

Operating expenses for the three months ended March 28, 2026 were $120 million, compared to $105 million for the three months ended March 29, 2025. The increase was primarily driven by higher research and development expenses as we continue to invest in our technology platform.

Net loss attributable to Symbotic Inc. for the three months ended March 28, 2026 was $42 million. Net loss per share attributable to Symbotic Inc. was $0.10 per diluted share.

Adjusted EBITDA for the three months ended March 28, 2026 was $25 million, compared to $18 million for the three months ended March 29, 2025.

Liquidity and Capital Resources: As of March 28, 2026, we had cash and cash equivalents of $820 million. We believe our existing cash and cash equivalents will be sufficient to meet our working capital and capital expenditure needs for at least the next twelve months.
